Modern Slavery Statement

Modern Slavery & Human Trafficking Statement

This statement is issued by the Praxis Global Alliance business unit of Praxian Global Private Limited (“Praxis Global Alliance”), on behalf of itself & its group companies pursuant to section 54 of the Modern Slavery Act 2015. Praxis Global Alliance has a zero-tolerance method to any form of modern slavery, forced labor, or human trafficking. We are committed to acting ethically & with honesty & transparency in all our business transactions.

About Praxis Global Alliance

Praxis Global Alliance is a Business unit of a global research & consulting firm headquartered in New Delhi, India, with over 100 employees globally.

Praxis Global Alliance works with C-suite to the frontline executives across business streams helping them with end-to-end business enablement, organizational transformation, & revenue maximization support in an agile environment.

Our Values

Praxis Global Alliance's corporate values: Showing integrity, cultivating people, & 'Client first always' are our core values that enable us to win & unleash the collective power of our network. Our Values form the standard against which we expect people to interact with colleagues, clients, & the companies we do business with.

By continually establishing our values, we hope to build an atmosphere where people are careful of their responsibilities & feel no pressure to report any concerns they have, including those involving modern slavery. We run a whistleblowing policy so that all employees know that they can raise concerns about how their colleagues are being treated, or put into practice within our business, without fear of comebacks.

Our Policies

Given the nature of our business as a service provider, we do not believe that there is any risk of slavery, human trafficking, or child labor prevailing within our business.

We operate internal policies to ensure that we are conducting business in an ethical manner which include:

Employees

All employees are provided with a contract of employment & our recruitment processes are in line with applicable employment laws. Praxis Global Alliance & its parent Praxian Global Private Limited offer market standard pay & rewards which are checked annually.

Suppliers

We make little use of services demanding unskilled labor & those services & products we do use are sourced from reputable suppliers. Our suppliers are for the most part limited to professional services, commercial real estate, staff benefits & healthcare, facility management, office equipment, & computer hardware & software. We believe that the risk of modern slavery in our supply chain is low, but we remain alert & will continue to review our processes, in respect of new & existing suppliers, & our internal policies.

Changes to this Policy: Please note that this policy may change from time to time. Please review the policy periodically for changes.
