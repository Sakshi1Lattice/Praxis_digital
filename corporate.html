Case Study

Context:

A PE investor wanted to understand the current strength & unique value propositions of a digital news & content platform. They also wanted us to assess the platform's feed quality & compare it with its competitors.

Approach:
  • Chose assigned language & assigned category / location for each user
  • Analyzed feed on the first day before any inputs by the user
  • For the next 10 days, tracked news related to aspecific category / location assigned
  • Analyzed feed content thrice a day to check for relevance & 'personalization'
  • Compared feed content across all the apps
Impact:

Successfully provided the client with a comprehensive report covering a detailed analysis of target's feed quality & identified its competitive advantage over its competitors
