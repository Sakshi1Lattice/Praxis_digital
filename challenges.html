Analytics Challenges

Top 10 Challenges Companies Face in Analytics

1. Insufficient Data: Through our analytics first approach, we define the most critical data requirements.
2. Disintegrated Data: We have a bouquet of integration services.
3. Data Quality: We use an array of tools for data cleaning & offer on-ground data collection services.
4. Cross Functional Silos: Through our consultative process, we onboard all critical stakeholders & get their buy-in.
5. Lack of Processes: We define the end-to-end process for analytics solution usage & pilot the same - permissions, users, tasks, approvals, etc.
6. Capability Gaps: We train organizations to consume analytics & use it in day-to-day decision making; we do not just handover a solution.
7. Lack of Outcome-Orientation: We look at the business problem first & then define the end outcome, features, usage, logic, & outputs before development.
8. Low Value Realization: Analytics requires patience for data sets & engines to mature. We have a sustained engagement model where we drive value realization.
9. Current Readiness (vs Future Readiness) of Data: We define course-corrective processes to ensure data accuracy in the future.
10. “Do it All at Once Attitude”: Our D-Cube® framework prioritizes analytics use cases in a phased approach to ensure results on-the-go.
